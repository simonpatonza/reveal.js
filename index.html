HTML Dropdowns, ngRepeat
& ngModel

When using a <select> you can populate the <option>'s in two ways


<select>
  <option ng-repeat="item in items">{{ item.text }}</option> 

This will not work either...
<select ng-options="item in items"></select>

This will
<select ng-model="selectedItem" ng-options="item in items">
</select>
